Hurricane/Typhoon Safety

What is a Hurricane/Typhoon?

Hurricanes and typhoons are powerful tropical storms that can bring heavy rain, strong winds, and flooding to coastal areas.

Safety Measures During a Hurricane/Typhoon

  • Move to a safe location or evacuate if instructed by authorities.
  • Stay indoors during the storm and away from windows.
  • Keep an emergency kit with food, water, medications, and important documents.

Real-Life Hurricane/Typhoon Cases

In 2013, Typhoon Haiyan devastated parts of the Philippines, causing widespread destruction and loss of life.
